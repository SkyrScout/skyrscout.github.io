Wendell Gabriel scored twice as FCI Levadia secured a 2–1 away victory over IFK Göteborg in the UEFA Conference League second qualifying round.

The Brazilian forward opened the scoring after 33 minutes before adding his second in first-half stoppage time, giving Levadia a 2–0 lead at the break at Gamla Ullevi.

IFK Göteborg pulled one back through a late own goal, but Levadia held on to take a 2–1 advantage into the return leg in Tallinn.
