title: "FC Basel submit final offer for Aaron Malouda"
---

Aaron Malouda now looks set for Switzerland rather than Scotland. According to @insidefcBasel, FC Basel have submitted a final offer to Sabah FC for the 20-year-old French winger, with the Azerbaijani club expected to accept.
