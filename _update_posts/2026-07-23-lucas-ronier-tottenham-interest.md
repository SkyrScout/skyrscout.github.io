Tottenham are among the clubs monitoring Coritiba winger Lucas Ronier, according to Sport Witness, citing ESPN Brazil journalist André Hernan. Fulham and clubs in Italy are also following the 21-year-old Brazilian.

Coritiba are reportedly in no rush to sell and believe they have one of the club's biggest talents on their hands. The Brazilian side want Ronier to make the right move into European football rather than simply accept the first major offer.

Ronier was featured by SkyrScout in March 2025 and was our top pick from that scouting batch. Our assessment highlighted his pace, left-footed ability from the right wing, dribbling and defensive contribution. In the Scout of This World podcast, we also noted that despite standing just 165 cm, he performed surprisingly well in aerial duels.
