---
title: "Mohamed El Amine Ramdaoui set to join Baltika Kaliningrad"
---

Mohamed El Amine Ramdaoui is set to join Russian Premier League side Baltika Kaliningrad. According to Africafoot, the 20-year-old Algerian striker will sign a three-year contract, with the transfer fee reportedly set at €1.5 million.

The Paradou AC forward is expected to travel to Russia to undergo his medical examination and finalize the transfer.
