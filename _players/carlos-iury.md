  Carlos Iury – 24-year-old left winger

  A direct, high-tempo winger primarily operating from the left. Standing 176 cm tall, Iury is lightning quick and thrives out wide. Although naturally right-footed, he is genuinely two-footed in delivery, capable of producing dangerous, accurate crosses from either side. His right foot is a clear weapon on set pieces — consistently delivering precise corners and generating serious power on knuckleball free-kicks. He is also confident striking from distance with both feet.

  Iury is a strong ball-carrier at speed and regularly looks to engage defenders 1v1. His game is more functional than aesthetic. His close control isn’t always clean, but it is largely effective, allowing him to beat his man and create end product. Complements this with a solid passing game and a clear attacking mindset.

  Areas for improvement:
  Ball control and first touch can be refined. Decision-making in the final third, choosing when to shoot versus when to combine, could be more consistent. With his tools, there is room to increase his goal output further.

  Career trajectory & current form:
  Developed through the Osvaldo Cruz FC academy (Brazil), Iury built his early senior career across Brazil’s regional and lower-tier competitions. In July 2024, he joined Singha Chiangrai United on a free transfer and quickly established himself as a reliable option in the Thai League 1. This season marks a clear step forward: 4 goals and 7 assists in his first 16 matches, his most productive spell to date.

  Transfer outlook:
  The Thai League ranks among the stronger competitions in AFC (currently 7th). With his contract expiring on 30 June 2026, Iury appears ready for a measured next step. His profile suggests suitability for a top-25 European league as a logical progression, where his pace, delivery, and set-piece threat could translate well.
