What is provisional in this playtest?

Football base: position/class fit remains provisional. Height only has a situational effect here: defenders can gain extra resistance against an aerial opponent. Missing facts give no effect.

YouTube: available long-form video data becomes a capped Momentum modifier. Missing metrics stay missing and produce no effect. Short views use a separate Buzz scale.

Defence: every computer-team attack is reduced by your drafted defenders. A defender used in your preceding attack contributes only half resistance on the immediate reply.

Scout: tiny prototype bonuses come from real report_by distribution. Bon Scout also gets a Short-creator bonus because all current Shorts are created by Bon Scout.

Data bridge: structured player facts can later be read from positions, height_cm, nationalities and game_traits. If a field is absent, the engine does nothing with it.
