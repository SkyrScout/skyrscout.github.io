Experimental prototype

SkyrScout Card Game Beta

Player cards are generated from SkyrScout profiles. Game strength will be based on the performance and popularity of each player's SkyrScout video — not our assessment of the player's football ability.

Step 1 of 2

Choose your Scout

Your Scout will affect the team later. In this Beta, the choice is saved for the draft but has no gameplay bonus yet.

What do I do? Choose one Scout. After that, the game will draw three player cards at a time.

Step 2 of 2 · Draft

Choose 1 of 3

Pick one player to add to your team.

How the draft works Three random players are drawn. Click Add to my team on ONE card. The other two are discarded, then three new players are drawn. Repeat until your five-player test team is complete.
